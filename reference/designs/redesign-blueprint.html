Layer 3

SEO entity architecture

The 25 locations and 20 services are the site's biggest hidden asset. Treating Locations, Doctors and Services as interlinked entities — not flat pages — is what wins local and AI search. Every entity should link out to the others it relates to.

Location/locations/[city]/
Doctors who practice here
Services offered at this clinic
Insurance plans accepted
Hours & directions geo + map
Reviews clinic-level ratings
Doctor/our-doctors/[name]/
Specialty e.g. adolescent medicine
Clinics locations they serve
Languages bilingual filter
Conditions treated / focus areas
Reviews provider-level
Schema & URL strategy

Structured data

Mark up clinics as MedicalClinic + LocalBusiness, providers as Physician, and FAQs/blog with FAQPage / Article.

Stable URLs

City-based location slugs and named doctor slugs (no IDs). Service pages at /services/[service]/ cross-link to offering clinics.

Interlinking

Every location lists its doctors & services; every doctor lists clinics & conditions. This mesh is the ranking engine.
